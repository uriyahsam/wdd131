Site Purpose

The purpose of the US HITECH website is to showcase the services offered in three core areas:

  • Technology: Web development and tech consulting for businesses.
  • Education: Online learning platform and home tutoring services.
  • E-commerce: Information about a coming online marketplace for local and digital goods.

The website will serve as a central hub for prospective clients, learners, and shoppers to explore the company's offerings and contact for services.

Color Schema

These colors will be used to give the website a clean, modern, and professional appearance:

  • Primary Color (#003566): Used for headings, navigation bar, and footer background.
  • Accent Color (#ffc300): Used for buttons, call-to-action sections, and highlights.

Typography

  • Segoe UI: Used for headings and section titles for a modern feel.
  • Verdana: Used for body text for better readability on screens.
